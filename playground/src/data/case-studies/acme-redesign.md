## The Challenge

Acme Corp's marketing team was stuck. Their WordPress site took 4+ seconds to load, required a developer for every landing page change, and the plugin stack was a security liability. They needed a modern stack that gave marketers autonomy without sacrificing performance.

## The Solution

The team migrated to Astro with MimsyCMS in a 3-week sprint. The developer built reusable section components (hero, features, testimonials, pricing), and the marketing team composed pages from those sections using the block builder. No code changes needed for new campaigns.

## The Results

The numbers speak for themselves. Page load dropped from 4.2 seconds to 0.8 seconds. Lighthouse scores went from 62 to 98. The marketing team now ships 12 content updates per week without any developer involvement, saving roughly 40 engineering hours per month.
