# Hello World

This is our first blog post, managed by MimsyCMS.

Welcome to the future of content management for Astro. We built MimsyCMS because every existing CMS felt like it was fighting against the way modern web teams actually work.

## Why Another CMS?

Most headless CMS tools require you to:
- Run a separate service
- Pay for a hosted solution
- Learn a new content model

MimsyCMS takes a different approach. Your content stays in your git repo as markdown and JSON files. The CMS is just an Astro integration that gives you a visual editing layer on top.

## What Makes It Different

The visual editor doesn't use a simulated preview. It renders your **actual site** and overlays editing controls directly on the DOM. When you click a heading, you're editing the real thing.
